FastCash Financial Services Limited was incorporated in Jamaica in 2009 and commenced operations in October 2014. The company is a subsidiary of the private equity investment company,Pon Di Rock Private Equity Limited. On June 15, 2022, FastCash Financial Services was listed on the Jamaica Stock Exchange following a historic IPO that was 8.5 times oversubscribed. “We lend” is part of FastCash’s ultimate goal to provide progressive financial opportunities to those with vision to succeed.
